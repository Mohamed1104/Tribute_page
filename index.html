The Career of Messi so far...

  • 1987 - June 24th Lionel Messi was born in Rosario Argentina.

  • 1993 - Aged six he was signed by Newell's Old boys in which his talents shone, he scored over 500 goals.

  • 1998 - Aged 11 Messi was diagnosed with growth hormone deficiency (GHD). This was a condition that stunted growth and required expensive medical treatment, including the use of the drug Human growth hormone.

  • 2000 - Messi aged 13 was scouted by Barcelona and was given a trial in which he impressed the coaches and was given a contract

  • 2004 - Aged 17 after rapidly rising through the ranks Messi was given his first team debut, he scored making him the youngest player to score a league goal.

  • 2006 - Messi won his first league title and Champions league

  • 2012 - Messi broke the broke the all-time world record for most goals scored in a calendar year. His final total of goals in 2012 was 91 – beating the previous record of 85 by German Gerd Muller, and Pele’s milestone of 75 in 1958.

  • 2014 - The world cup in Brazil, Messi almost singlehandedly dragged Argentina to the final narrowly missing out on the cup to Germany however he was given the golden ball which is given to the best player in the tournament.

  • 2021 - Finally Messi was able to give his home country a trophy in winning the Copa America he finished the tournament with the most goals and assists.
